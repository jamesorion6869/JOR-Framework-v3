# Contributing to JOR-Framework-v3

Thank you for your interest in contributing! This repository is primarily for research and reporting purposes. To keep interactions focused and productive, please follow these guidelines:

- **Issues**: Use issues for questions, feedback, or suggestions related to the framework.
- **Pull Requests**: Submit pull requests only for corrections, fixes, or improvements to existing content.
- **Off-topic discussions**: Please avoid unrelated conversations; keep the repo focused on the research and framework.

All contributions are subject to the [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
